Why It Matters为何重要

History Meets Fiction七实三虚的历史叙事

The novel is celebrated for its blend of history and fiction—described as "seven parts fact, three parts fiction" (七实三虚). This created a work that feels historically authentic while being dramatically compelling.这部小说以其历史与虚构的独特融合而闻名——清代史学家张学成称之为"七实三虚"。这种方法使作品既有历史的真实感，又具有戏剧的感染力。

天下大势，分久必合，合久必分。

— Opening lines— 《三国演义》开篇

Politics and Ambition政治与道德的深刻探讨

The novel explores political legitimacy, the relationship between politics and morality, and the conflict between Confucian idealism and Legalist pragmatism.小说深刻探讨了政治合法性、政治与道德的关系，以及儒家理想主义与法家实用主义之间的冲突。

Why It Endures为何经久不衰

Timeless Appeal永恒的魅力

Cross-Cultural Legacy跨文化的永恒魅力

The novel has achieved "canonical status" across East Asia. In Korea, it's called "the national novel," with Yi Mun-yol's translation selling over 20 million copies.这部小说在东亚享有"经典地位"。在韩国，它被称为"国民小说"，李文烈的韩译本销量超过两千万册。

Language and Culture语言与文化的深远影响

Many Chinese proverbs derive from the novel, including "Speak of Cao Cao and Cao Cao arrives" (说曹操，曹操到).许多中国谚语源自此书，如"说曹操，曹操到"。
