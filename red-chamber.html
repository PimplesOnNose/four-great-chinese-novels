About the Novel小说简介

Author

Cao Xueqin曹雪芹

He completed the first 80 chapters; the final 40 were added by Gao E and Cheng Weiyuan.前八十回为曹雪芹所著，后四十回由高鹗和程伟元续写。

Period

18th Century · Qing Dynasty18世纪 · 清代

Written during the mid-Qing dynasty, reflecting the author's own family's rise and fall.成书于清代中期，反映了作者自己家族的兴衰。

Setting

Qing Dynasty · Prospect Garden清代 · 大观园

Centered on the Jia family mansion and the beautiful Prospect Garden in Beijing.以贾府和北京美丽的大观园为中心。

Scale

120回 · 百万余字

The longest of the four novels, with 447 named characters.四大名著中最长的一部，有四百四十七个有名有姓的人物。

Historical Significance历史意义

Why It Matters为何重要

The Pinnacle of Chinese Literature中国文学的巅峰之作

Widely regarded as the greatest of the four classic novels. Wang Guowei called it "the tragedy of tragedies" and "worthy of being the one great masterpiece in the realm of Chinese art."被广泛认为是四大名著中最伟大的一部。王国维称之为"悲剧中的悲剧"，是"中国艺术领域中唯一伟大的杰作"。

假作真时真亦假，无为有处有还无。

— Truth becomes fiction when the fiction's true— 《红楼梦》太虚幻境联

Birth of Redology红学的诞生

The novel gave rise to Redology (红学), an entire scholarly field. Mao Zedong read it five times and praised it as one of China's greatest works.这部小说催生了"红学"这一专门的学术领域。毛泽东读了五遍，称赞其为中国最伟大的文学作品之一。

Why It Endures为何经久不衰

Timeless Appeal永恒的魅力

Eternal Exploration of Human Nature永恒的人性探索

The tragic romance between Jia Baoyu and Lin Daiyu has become one of the most beloved love stories in world literature.贾宝玉与林黛玉的爱情悲剧已成为世界文学中最动人的爱情故事之一。

Artistic Achievement艺术成就与文化传承

Unprecedented psychological depth, encyclopedic depiction of Chinese culture, and masterful use of poetry.前所未有的心理深度，百科全书式的中国文化描绘，以及精湛的诗词运用。
